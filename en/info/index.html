Have Something to Say?

Lightning Talks

Do you have something exciting to share but missed the submission deadline? Welcome to participate in SITCON 2026 Lightning Talks! Lightning Talks provide eight slots for the quickest attendees to share their projects or ideas in five minutes!

On the day of the conference from 10:00 AM to 3:30 PM, you are welcome to submit your proposal at the designated whiteboard area. The submission method is simple! Once submission opens, choose an empty slot on the whiteboard and write down your talk title and name (e.g., 'How I Became the Wind by Windful'). There are only eight slots, and each person is limited to one submission, so be quick! After submitting, please scan the form next to the whiteboard to provide more information, such as title, speaker name, and outline. We suggest preparing this content in advance to ensure a smooth process!

If you successfully become a speaker, please gather at the right side of R0 on the 3rd floor at 4:40 PM sharp with your presentation equipment. Agenda team members will be there to assist you. To maintain diversity and fun, we do not accept overly commercial submissions. If your talk exceeds the time limit or contains commercial content for more than ten seconds, the screen will be cut off directly!

We invite you to this adrenaline-pumping activity to communicate and share with more people at SITCON!
