Conference Activities

Conference Program

Keynote

This session takes you straight to the heart of this year’s conference theme. We have invited a speaker to break down the core ideas in a clear and accessible way. Through the speaker’s perspective, you will not only understand what SITCON hopes to convey this year, but also follow the rhythm of the talk to brainstorm fresh ideas and uncover deeper insights together.

Panel

This is a deep and engaging conversation featuring one moderator and three panelists. Guided by the moderator’s thoughtful facilitation, guests from diverse backgrounds will explore this year’s theme from different angles. You will hear multiple perspectives collide on stage, helping you step outside the box, re-examine the core issues from new standpoints, and piece together a broader and richer technical vision through shared experience.

Sessions

Here you will find excellent talks submitted by students from many backgrounds. Whether you prefer in-depth Presentations that thoroughly unpack technical concepts, or fast-paced Espresso sessions that deliver key takeaways in a short time, there is plenty to discover. On top of that, there are flexible open-format sessions where ideas from different backgrounds can freely interweave. Come experience the practical knowledge and boundless creativity brought by student speakers on-site!

Demo Showcase

Building on last year’s enthusiastic response, the Demo Showcase returns with an exciting upgrade! This special exhibition area goes beyond the regular program, providing a stage for students to present their projects and research through live demos and conversations. It is a great opportunity for attendees to explore diverse topics in a relaxed way and spark new exchanges and inspiration.

The showcase will take place on March 28, 2026, the day of the conference, at the booths on the north side of the 4th floor, featuring outstanding student work from across Taiwan. Whether you are interested in technical innovation, project sharing, interactive design, or cutting-edge research discoveries, you are sure to find topics that surprise and inspire you.

In addition, project exhibitors will be present at different time slots to chat and discuss with attendees. This is a perfect chance to ask questions, learn, and meet amazing peers from around the country. If you love exploring new knowledge, don’t miss it!

First Time Here & Want to Make Friends

Compass Project

Looking forward to meeting like-minded peers at the conference? We sincerely invite you to join the Compass Project!

The Compass Project provides an opportunity for exchange, helping you find partners with shared interests to explore topics together. Whether you are an experienced participant or a curious newcomer, you can find resonance here.

Based on the information you provide, we will match you with suitable partners and form groups. We also encourage everyone to actively interact with their matched partners. To ensure a good experience for all, please stay in touch with your group members after signing up and participate actively, so that every participant looking forward to interaction can truly meet others. During the conference, you can join various activities with your group members or have a relaxed meetup during lunch. Whether you share experiences, exchange viewpoints, or discuss conference sessions in depth, it will be a rewarding experience.

No matter whether this is your first time here or you already know the IT field well, everyone can find their place here and work together to create an open environment for mutual exchange and learning. We look forward to your participation and to making SITCON 2026 even more exciting together!

Guided Tours

A scene of tangled chaos lies before you. In a melody of interwoven crowds, perhaps you still feel a little lost; the laughter throughout the venue is like scattered notes with no fixed beat. What are you waiting for? Join a Guided Tour! Let our conductors guide you through the unknowns of SITCON and help you find your own melody and sense of belonging.

At the tour meeting point, you will find guides holding SITCON flags. Follow the SITCON guides as they lead you to community booths and the Demo Showcase, helping you understand the many facets of SITCON and how the conference works.

Want to Play Something at the Venue

Reality Game

Don’t know what to do between sessions? Want to interact with booths but don’t know how to start the conversation? Don’t worry—open OPass and join the Reality Game!

This year’s Reality Game combines the SITCON 2026 theme, “Jam the Chaos.” In the game, you will unlock levels, challenge memory sequence games, master the rhythm, solve puzzles, accumulate completions, and climb the leaderboard for a chance to win discount vouchers for the SITCON 2026 souvenir booth!

【How to Unlock New Levels?】

🤝 Interact with Sponsors: Talk with sponsors to unlock new levels, verify higher-difficulty challenges, and sprint up the leaderboard!

🎤 Join Activities & Explore the Venue: Explore the venue, check in at activity locations, and participate in exciting events. Each activity and new location can unlock a new level~

🔍 Meet New Friends: Interact with other attendees; every new encounter can unlock a new level.

The more levels you complete and the higher you rank, the greater your chances of winning. Pick up your phone and groove with the rhythm!

Whiteboard War

In this era of intertwined viewpoints and boundless creativity, pick up a whiteboard marker, add your thoughts to this chaotic world, and begin a wonderful clash of ideas!

We sincerely invite you to the whiteboard area on the 3rd floor to join this “Whiteboard War” without smoke or gunpowder. Jam the code together, debate, and defend the sect you believe in (x). This will be a grand platform for you to showcase creativity and let viewpoints collide.

In a relaxed atmosphere, let us use whiteboards as weapons, unleash boundless creativity, challenge different viewpoints, and create more sparks! Come splash your ideas across the board and make this whiteboard world even more chaotic!

Have Something to Say?

Meet the Speakers

Just finished an inspiring session, but still have questions you did not get to ask? Want to talk with the speakers about the stories and experiences behind their work? Don’t take those thoughts home unanswered!

Meet the Speakers is a great opportunity to have face-to-face conversations with speakers. After their sessions, some speakers will move to Hall S and wait there to chat with attendees. Whether you want to ask deeper questions about the session, discuss technical details, or simply exchange experiences, you are welcome to join.

The activity will take place from 15:25 to 16:45 in Hall S. Each speaker is expected to stay for 20 to 30 minutes. No preparation is required. Find the speaker you are interested in and say hello!

Lightning Talks

Do you have something exciting to share, but missed the submission deadline? Welcome to SITCON 2026 Lightning Talks! Lightning Talks provide eight slots for the fastest attendees to share their projects or ideas with everyone in five minutes.

On the day of the conference, from 10:10 AM to 3:30 PM, you are welcome to submit your proposal at the designated whiteboard area. The submission method is simple: once submissions open, choose an empty slot on the whiteboard and write down your talk title and name (for example, “How I Became the Wind by Windful”). There are only eight slots, and each person may submit only once, so act fast! After submitting, please scan the form next to the whiteboard to provide more information, such as your title, speaker name, and outline. We suggest preparing this content in advance to ensure a smooth process.

If you successfully become a speaker, please gather at the right side of R0 on the 3rd floor at 4:40 PM sharp and bring your presentation equipment. Members of the Agenda Team will be there to assist you. To maintain diversity and fun, we do not accept overly commercial submissions. If your talk runs over time or includes commercial content for more than ten seconds, the screen will be cut immediately.

We invite you to join this adrenaline-pumping activity and share your ideas with more people at SITCON!
