Top Referrer

The top five referrers with the most successful referrals will receive exclusive honors and SITCON 2026 commemorative gifts, and will be publicly recognized and awarded at the SITCON 2026 conference!

Ranking and Prize Collection:

  • Ranked by number of successful referrals, top five will be selected.
  • If referral counts are the same, the one with the earlier 'first successful referral time' takes precedence.

Fairness and Notices

To ensure fairness, the organizer will:

  • Exclude referral records generated from duplicate, abnormal, or false registrations
  • Reserve the right to cancel eligibility if behavior affecting fairness is found
  • All referral and lottery records are based on system data

The organizer reserves the right of final interpretation and adjustment of event content, prizes, and rules.
